Cucumber Salad

2 Large Cucumbers
1 Large Tomato
1 Medium Onion
2 Cups Italian Dressin
2 teaspoons Seasoning Salt
2 teaspoons Salt
1 teaspoon Pepper

Thinly slice cucumbers, tomato and onion into medium bowl.  Add in Italian Dressing, salt, pepper and seasoning salt.
Let sit overnight in refrigerator.
Serve cold.
